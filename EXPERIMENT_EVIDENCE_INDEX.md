- **Scripts:** `scripts/train_safety_guided_defense.py`, `scripts/build_safety_guided_comparison.py`
- **Logs & metadata:** `results/safety_guided_defense/seed42/logs/seed42_variant{A,B,C,D}_*_training_log.csv` and `..._metadata.json` (per-epoch SafetyScore, false-alarm burden, dual-selection flags, and `raw_ungated_safety_best`).
- **Test-eval artifacts:** `results/safety_guided_defense/seed42/test_eval/` — single-ε and 18-ε FGSM/PGD sweep CSVs for all 8 selected checkpoints (variants A–D × {bySafetyScore, byValMacroF1}).
- **Checkpoints:** `checkpoints/safety_guided_defense/seed42/*.pt` — **local-only (gitignored)**.
- **Commit:** `f9e5ac5 Add seed42 safety-proxy-guided defense pilot` (159 files).
- **Key result (test n=500, ε=0.030):**
  - Existing FGSM defense PGD@0.030 fall recall **0.089**.
  - Safety-guided variants improve PGD@0.030 fall recall to **~0.44–0.49**.
  - **Variant D** (FGSM+PGD multi-ε, fall-weighted, safety-guided + guard) is **best-balanced**: PGD recall 0.444, clean fall recall 1.000, clean accuracy 0.746, widest robust ε-range.
  - **Variant C** has higher raw PGD recall (0.489) but **worse clean accuracy (0.642) and more false alarms (190)**.
  - The **clean-collapse guard was required**; without it the raw SafetyScore selects degenerate always-fall predictors (clean acc ≈0.09).
